2)In two or three sentences explain the difference between a “do while” and a “while” loop?
"while" checks the condition first then determine to excute the sequence and check the condition again. But, "do while" excutes the sequence first, then check the condition to determine if it should excute the sequence again. Basically, it does the sequence first then works as "while".

3)In two or three sentences explain what is meant by the term conditional expression? 
The form is "condition? operand1 : operand2". It checks the "condition" first, then if it is true, it uses "operand1", otherwise, it uses "operand2"
